Health Tips for Underweight Individuals

Causes of Being Underweight

Being underweight can be due to a variety of reasons including genetics, high metabolism, lack of food intake, or underlying medical conditions.

Tips to Gain Healthy Weight

  • Eat more frequently and choose nutrient-rich foods.
  • Include a variety of food groups in your meals.
  • Opt for whole-grain bread, pasta, and cereals.
  • Drink smoothies or shakes.
  • Exercise regularly to build muscle mass.

Sample Diet Plan

Here is a sample diet plan for gaining weight in a healthy way:

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with fruits and nuts, a glass of milk.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken, quinoa, and a side of vegetables.
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and almonds.
  • Dinner: Salmon, brown rice, and steamed broccoli.
