Tips to regulate heart rate

  • Exercise: Engaging in regular physical activity, such as walking, jogging, cycling, or swimming, helps improve cardiovascular fitness and can regulate heart rate.
  • Manage Stress: Practice stress-reducing techniques like deep breathing exercises, meditation, yoga, or mindfulness to help keep your heart rate in check.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drinking enough water helps maintain healthy blood volume and can prevent dehydration-related heart rate issues.
  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Excessive caffeine and alcohol can lead to irregular heart rates. Moderation is key.
  • Avoid Smoking: Smoking can increase heart rate and blood pressure. Quitting smoking is beneficial for heart health.
