Tips to improve sleep quality

Causes for having poor sleep

Being sleep deprived can result from factors such as poor diet, Stress and Anxiety, Poor Sleep Hygiene, or Late-Night Eating.

Tips to improve sleep

  • Go to Bed and Wake Up at the Same Time.
  • Create a Relaxing Bedtime Routine.
  • Optimize Your Sleep Environment.
  • Limit Screen Time Before Bed.
  • Maintain a Healthy Lifestyle.

Sample Diet Plan for improving sleep

Diet can play a significant role in improving sleep quality. Here's a guide to foods and dietary habits that can help promote better sleep

  • Tryptophan-Rich Foods like Turkey, Chicken and dairy products.
  • Melatonin-Rich Foods like cherries and grapes
  • Magnesium-Rich Foods like almonds,spinach and leaves
