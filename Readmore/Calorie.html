2. Water intake

Tips to be hydrated

  • Drink Regularly Throughout the Day.
  • Start Your Day with Water.
  • Eat Water-Rich Foods.
  • Limit Caffeine and Alcohol.
  • Adjust your water intake according to activities.
  • Watch for Signs of Dehydration.

3.Cycling

Tips to improve fitness through cycling

  • Set Clear Goals: Define Objectives and Set specific goals
  • Create a Consistent Routine: Schedule Rides and cycle regularly.
  • Start Gradually: If you're new to cycling or getting back into it, start with shorter rides and gradually increase distance and intensity.
  • Prioritize Recovery Allow adequate time for rest and recovery between rides to prevent overtraining and promote muscle repair.
